02 · What it does

Every feature,
built for builders.

Everything you need to version-control your Shortcuts library.

📸
Snapshot Archiving
Capture any shortcut with a description, tags, action count, and iCloud link in seconds.
🔍
Change Detection
Compares action count against the last archive — warns you if nothing has changed.
⏪
Version Restore
Browse full version history and reinstall any previous version from its iCloud link.
🏷️
Collections
Organise shortcuts into named collections — Production, Dev, Experiments, or custom.
🔎
Smart Search
Find shortcuts by name across your entire library, including dictation-powered search.
🔄
Auto Update Check
Silently checks RoutineHub on launch and notifies you when an update is available.
03 · How it works

Simple by design.
Powerful under the hood.

Scriptable handles the intelligence. Shortcuts handles the native iOS actions.

1
Select a Shortcut
Choose from Favorites, browse by folder, or search your entire library.
2
Change Detection Runs
Instantly compares against the last saved version — no extra steps needed.
3
Add Description & Tags
Give the archive a meaningful description and optional tags.
4
Archive is Saved
Versioned snapshot stored locally — iCloud link, action count, timestamp, and metadata.
5
Restore Any Time
Browse history and tap Restore to reinstall any previous version from its iCloud link.
